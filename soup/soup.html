Ingredients for Chicken-Escarole Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 1/2-ounce) can Italian-style-style stewed tomatoes, undrained and chopped
  • 1 (14-ounce) can fat-free, less-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cups coarse chopped escarole (about 1 small head)
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and broth in a large saucepan
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Add chicken, escarole, and oil; cook 5 minutes
